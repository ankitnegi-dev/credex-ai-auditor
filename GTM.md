# Go-To-Market Strategy: 100 Users in 30 Days

## Target User
Startup founders and engineering leads at seed to Series A companies (5–50 employees) who pay for multiple AI tools but have never audited their combined spend.

## Week 1 — Community Seeding (Days 1–7)
- Post in r/startups, r/entrepreneur, r/SideProject on Reddit with title: "I built a free tool that shows how much your startup wastes on AI subscriptions"
- Share in Indie Hackers "Show IH" section with full breakdown of the build
- Post in relevant Slack communities: Indie Hackers, YC Alumni, Product Hunt Makers
- Target: 20–30 signups from organic community posts

## Week 2 — Product Hunt Launch (Days 8–14)
- Submit to Product Hunt as a free tool — schedule for Tuesday morning (peak traffic)
- Prepare hunter outreach: ask 3–5 active PH hunters to support the launch
- Write a compelling tagline: "Find out how much your startup wastes on AI tools — in 60 seconds"
- Target: 40–60 upvotes, 15–25 new users from PH traffic

## Week 3 — Direct Outreach (Days 15–21)
- Cold DM 50 startup founders on Twitter/LinkedIn with personalised message:
  "Hey [name], I noticed you use [tool] — I built a free audit tool that shows if you're overpaying. Takes 60 seconds. Want to try it?"
- Share audit link in startup WhatsApp groups and Telegram communities
- Reach out to 5 startup-focused newsletters for a free mention
- Target: 20–30 users from direct outreach

## Week 4 — Content & Referral (Days 22–30)
- Publish blog post: "We analysed 50 startup AI bills — here's where the money goes"
- Add "Share your audit" button to results page to drive viral sharing
- Email all captured leads with: "Your audit is ready to share — send it to a founder friend"
- Target: 15–20 users from content + referral loop

## Total Target: 100+ users in 30 days

## Key Metrics to Track
- Audits completed per day
- Email capture rate (target: >40% of audit completions)
- Share rate (target: >15% of users share their audit URL)
- Return visit rate

## Credex Positioning
After showing savings, add a CTA: "Save even more with Credex credits — one subscription for all your AI tools." This converts audit users into Credex leads.
